Detailing FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas of North Jersey do you service?

Royalty Details is a mobile auto detailing service covering Bergen, Hudson, Essex, and Passaic counties — including Hackensack, Paramus, Jersey City, Hoboken, Newark, Clifton, Paterson, Montclair, Wayne, and surrounding North Jersey towns.

How much does a ceramic coating cost?

Ceramic coating pricing depends on vehicle size, paint condition, and the level of prep required. Contact us at 551-340-7923 for a custom quote — most ceramic coating jobs include paint decontamination and a polish before application.

Do you come to my house or office?

Yes — Royalty Details is a fully mobile service. We bring our equipment, water, and power directly to your driveway, garage, or office parking lot anywhere in North Jersey.

How long does a full detail take?

A standard exterior detail takes 2–3 hours. Full interior + exterior runs 4–5 hours. Paint correction and ceramic coating jobs typically take a full day or longer depending on the vehicle's condition.

How often should I get my car detailed?

For most daily-driven cars in North Jersey, we recommend a full detail every 3–4 months to keep paint protected from road salt, UV, and contaminants. Ceramic coatings extend that protection to 6–12 months between full details.

What's the difference between paint correction and a regular polish?

Paint correction is a multi-stage process that physically removes swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation from the clear coat — restoring a true mirror finish. A regular polish only enhances gloss without removing defects. Paint correction is the recommended prep before any ceramic coating.
