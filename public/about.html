The Story

Detailing the way it should be done.

Royalty Details was founded in North Jersey on a simple idea — every car, no matter the make or model, deserves to be treated like it belongs in a showroom. We started with a passion for vehicles and grew into a mobile studio trusted by drivers across the region.

We don't cut corners. We don't rush. We treat every vehicle like it's our own.
